# Simon Hedley

Simon Hedley is a strategic advisor, author, and The Strategic Alchemist™ with over 30 years experience growing businesses and supporting founders across multiple industries.

He is the author of two published books — The Five Connections™ (2025) and Chaos To Clarity™ (2023) — and the founder of The Simple Idea Ltd.

For over two decades Simon worked primarily by referral and behind the scenes, known to those who knew him as Simon X™. He is described by those who have worked with him as The Secret Weapon for Good People Doing Great Things.

## The Body of Work

Simon Hedley has built two complete frameworks — one for the internal architecture of a person, one for the external architecture of a business.

**Chaos To Clarity™** — the internal architecture. A ten-step progression from the reality of chaos to the possibility of clarity, built around The Key Question™: "Do I know what really matters to me now?"

**The Five Connections™** — the external architecture. A complete operating framework for building the five types of relationships that drive business growth and sustainable scaling: Strategic Alliances, Joint Ventures, Mastermind Alliances, Inner Circle Connections, and Raving Fans.

Together they form a complete system. Inner work first. Outer work follows.
