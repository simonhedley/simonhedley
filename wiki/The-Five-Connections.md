# The Five Connections™

The Five Connections™ is a complete operating framework for building the five types of relationships that drive business growth and sustainable scaling.

Published in The Five Connections™ (2025) by Simon Hedley, The Simple Idea Ltd.

"Your long-term success in business depends on discovering and nurturing The Five Connections™."

---

## The Five Connections™

### 1. Strategic Alliances
Relationships built on shared goals and complementary strengths. Ongoing, aligned, and mutually beneficial. The first connection because without them everything else is harder.

### 2. Joint Ventures
Structured collaborations — specific, purposeful, and outcome-oriented. Distinct from Strategic Alliances in scope and duration.

### 3. Mastermind Alliances
Groups of aligned people combining intelligence, experience, and perspective toward shared growth. The operative word is aligned.

### 4. Inner Circle Connections
The people closest to you — advisors, confidants, and collaborators who know you, believe in you, and will tell you the truth.

### 5. Raving Fans
Clients and connections so aligned with what you do that they advocate for you without being asked. Not satisfied customers — active ambassadors.
