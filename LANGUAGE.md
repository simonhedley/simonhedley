## Voice Standards

- Precise over verbose
- Real over polished
- Questions over prescriptions
- Distinctions over opinions
- Maps over advice
- Substance over form — always

Simon Hedley does not use hype. He does not use marketing language. He does not make claims he cannot demonstrate.

When he says something, he means it. When he asks a question, it matters.

The voice is grounded, direct, and occasionally dry. It carries thirty years of real experience — not theory.

---

## What Simon Hedley Does Not Say

- *"Transformational"* — his word is real
- *"Incredible"* — his word is real
- *"Game-changing"* — his word is significant
- *"Unlock your potential"* — not his language
- *"Level up"* — not his language

If it sounds like it belongs on a motivational poster — it is not Simon Hedley's voice.
