## The Five Connections™ Colour Codes

A live relationship classification system built on traffic light and RAG status principles. Assigns one of five colours to each person in your ecosystem to gauge trust, alignment, and collaboration potential.

Developed with military and special forces veterans, top security advisors, and private equity and banking experts.

| Colour | Status | Meaning |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 🔴 Red | Stop / Avoid | People who drain your time, energy, or resources |
| 🟠 Amber | Caution / Potential | Connections requiring more due diligence |
| 🟢 Green | Go / Solid Fit | People who align with your values and goals |
| 🔵 Blue | Active Collaboration | Trusted allies actively engaged with you |
| 🟣 Purple | VIP / Inner Circle | Your most valued, highest-trust connections |

**Rule One:** *Assume Nothing and Confirm Everything.*

### Clients vs. Customers
- *"Customers are transactional."*
- *"Clients value the relationship and see you as a partner in achieving their goals."*

### Partners vs. Partnerships
Formal legal partner versus informal collaborative partner. Clarity on this distinction protects against misunderstanding and unintended legal obligations.

### Substance Over Form
*"What is really going on — not what does it say in the paperwork."*
Drawn from UK accounting principles. Applied to agreements, relationships, and integrity.

### Force Multipliers
People, relationships, or actions that fundamentally change the game.

### Win-Win-Win
Three wins, not two. The non-negotiable outcomes standard applied across all relationships and agreements.
